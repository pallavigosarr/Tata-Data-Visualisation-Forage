# Tata Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of Tata's Data Visualisation Virtual Experience Program on Forage.

The objective was to analyze retail sales data, create meaningful visualizations, and present business insights that would help senior leadership make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

The company needed clear and actionable insights from its sales data to support strategic decision-making. Management wanted to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and geographic performance.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze sales transaction data.
* Identify business trends and patterns.
* Design effective visualizations.
* Communicate insights to executive stakeholders.
* Support decision-making using data storytelling.

---

## Tasks Completed

### Task 1: Framing Business Scenarios

* Reviewed business requirements.
* Identified key stakeholder questions.
* Determined relevant performance metrics.

### Task 2: Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Examined data quality.
* Removed invalid transactions.
* Prepared data for visualization.

### Task 3: Data Visualization

Created dashboards and charts to analyze:

* Revenue trends
* Country-wise performance
* Customer purchasing behavior
* Top-performing markets
* Monthly sales patterns

### Task 4: Executive Presentation

* Summarized findings.
* Presented business recommendations.
* Communicated insights tailored to CEO and CMO requirements.

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI

## Dashboard Highlights

* Revenue Analysis
* Geographic Sales Distribution
* Customer Segmentation

---

## Key Insights

* Certain countries contributed significantly to overall revenue.
* Sales performance varied across geographic regions.
* Seasonal trends influenced customer purchasing behavior.
* Data visualization enabled quick identification of growth opportunities.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
* Explore expansion opportunities in emerging markets.
* Monitor sales trends regularly using dashboards.
* Use data-driven reporting to support strategic planning.
